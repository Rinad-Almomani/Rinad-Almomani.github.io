Classes

Discover our diverse range of fitness classes.

  • Cardio Class

    • Cardio exercise is an important component of a healthy fitness routine. It helps to increase your heart rate, burn calories and keep your body in shape. There are many types of cardio exercises available, from running, jogging and walking, to swimming and biking. Cardio can also be done with equipment such as stair-climbers, rowing machines, elliptical trainers and stationary bikes.

  • Yoga Class

    • Yoga classes combine stretching, strengthening, and relaxation techniques. The goal of yoga is to improve balance, flexibility, range of motion, and strength.

  • Zumba Class

    • Zumba is a very famous fitness class type, as many people enjoy this mix of music, martial art and resistance training.

  • Pilats Class

    • Pilates is a form of exercise that focuses on low-impact movements, stretching, and strengthening the muscles of the core, arms, and legs.It was developed by Joseph Pilates in the early 20th century and has become one of the most popular forms of exercise today. Pilates is especially beneficial for those looking to improve posture, flexibility, and balance.

  • HIIT Class

    • High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) is a form of fitness that alternates between high-intensity and low-intensity exercises. It can be performed as circuit training, or using body weight or machines. It is designed to be fast-paced and push the body to its limits. HIIT is often used as an effective way to burn fat and build strength in a shorter amount of time than with traditional strength training.
